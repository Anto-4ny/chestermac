Our Services

Business Registration & Tax Compliance Solutions

At Chestermac Consultants, we simplify business operations with expert services in compliance, registration, and taxation to help you focus on growth while we handle the paperwork.

Business Registration

Business name search & registration, CR12 processing, company updates, change of directors, and annual filings.

KRA Services

KRA PIN registration, tax obligation updates, non-filers handling, and general KRA support & troubleshooting.

Tax Compliance

Tax compliance certificate applications, tax returns filing, rental tax advisory, and PAYE payroll management.

Accounting & Advisory

Bookkeeping, financial statement preparation, tax advisory for individuals & corporates, and audit readiness.

NCA Registration

National Construction Authority (NCA) registration and licensing services for contractors and construction businesses.

Tax Agency Services

We act on your behalf for assessments, returns, dispute resolution, and all KRA-related compliance issues.
